android screen recording with adb shell screenrecord

Fri 12 March 2021 
[root@localhost ~]# adb devices
List of devices attached
65b9a5b4    device

default starting recording at my oneplus6's screen resolution 1080x2280 pixels for 180 seconds(3 mins) with bit rate 20Mbps.

[root@localhost ~]# adb shell screenrecord /sdcard/test.mp4
^C

now we can change it. 4000000 means 4M

[root@localhost ~]# adb shell screenrecord --size 1280x720 --bit-rate 4000000 /sdcard/test.mp4
^C

unstable usage --rotate , may not work. just try, maybe your phone has no problem.another --bugreport may give u info of what is wrong. the last important is --time-limit=n, and its value is n second(s). we can see more info at terminal with --verbose.

[root@localhost ~]# adb shell screenrecord --time-limit=3600 /sdcard/test.mp4
Time limit 3600s outside acceptable range [1,180]
