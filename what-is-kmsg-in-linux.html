what is kmsg in linux

Fri 20 November 2020

let's look at /dev/kmsg

[root@localhost ~]# ls -lhZ /dev/kmsg
crw-r--r--. 1 root root system_u:object_r:kmsg_device_t:s0 1, 11 Nov  3 19:58 /dev/kmsg

and then look at /proc/kmsg

[root@localhost ~]# ls -lhZ /proc/kmsg
-r--------. 1 root root system_u:object_r:proc_kmsg_t:s0 0 Nov  3 19:58 /proc/kmsg
